# Luthien Tagline Brainstorm - Round 2

## Filtering Criteria
- Must differentiate from general LLM observability (LangSmith, Helicone)
- Must speak to AI CODING specifically
- Should evoke trust, control, or peace of mind
- Should be memorable (short, rhythmic, or contrasting)
- Should work for both individual devs and teams

---

## Refined Options

### Category A: Control Without Restriction
The challenge: "control" can sound limiting. Reframe as empowerment.

1. **Let AI code. Stay in control.** (6 words)
   - Pro: Clear tension, resolved
   - Con: "Control" might sound limiting

2. **AI-powered coding. Human-defined rules.** (5 words)
   - Pro: Clear division of labor
   - Con: "Rules" sounds bureaucratic

3. **Your AI codes. Your rules apply.** (6 words)
   - Pro: Ownership emphasis
   - Con: Slightly awkward rhythm

### Category B: Visibility/Transparency
The promise: See what's happening, no black box.

4. **See everything your AI agent does.** (6 words)
   - Pro: Clear, simple promise
   - Con: Generic, could be any monitoring tool

5. **Two env vars. Total visibility.** (5 words)
   - Pro: Concrete, memorable, low friction
   - Con: Technical, might not land emotionally

6. **Drop in. See everything.** (4 words)
   - Pro: Ultra-simple, action-oriented
   - Con: Doesn't mention AI/coding specifically

### Category C: Trust/Confidence
The emotion: Move from anxiety to confidence.

7. **AI coding agents you can actually trust.** (7 words)
   - Pro: Trust is the core need
   - Con: "Actually" feels defensive

8. **Trust your AI. Verify everything.** (5 words)
   - Pro: "Trust but verify" echo
   - Con: Might sound paranoid

9. **Ship AI-assisted code. Sleep at night.** (6 words)
   - Pro: Strong outcome + emotion
   - Con: "Sleep at night" is cliche

10. **Code fearlessly with AI.** (4 words)
    - Pro: Echoes Fly.io, empowering
    - Con: "Fearlessly" might imply current fear

### Category D: Infrastructure Positioning
Position as essential layer, not optional tool.

11. **The control plane for AI coding agents.** (7 words)
    - Pro: Technical credibility, infrastructure positioning
    - Con: "Control plane" is jargon

12. **Safety infrastructure for AI-assisted development.** (6 words)
    - Pro: Clear, professional
    - Con: "Safety" might feel corporate/compliance

13. **The missing layer between you and your AI.** (8 words)
    - Pro: Identifies a gap
    - Con: Too long

### Category E: Action-Oriented
Short, punchy, memorable.

14. **See. Control. Ship.** (3 words)
    - Pro: Ultra-memorable, workflow
    - Con: Generic

15. **Watch. Enforce. Trust.** (3 words)
    - Pro: Clear progression
    - Con: "Enforce" sounds heavy-handed

16. **Observe. Govern. Deploy.** (3 words)
    - Pro: Professional
    - Con: "Govern" is enterprise-y

---

## Round 2 Top 5

1. **Let AI code. Stay in control.** - Best balance of clarity and emotion
2. **Two env vars. Total visibility.** - Concrete differentiator (drop-in)
3. **Ship AI-assisted code. Sleep at night.** - Strong outcome
4. **The control plane for AI coding agents.** - Infrastructure positioning
5. **See. Control. Ship.** - Memorable action trio

---

## What's Missing

- None of these capture the "for power users" angle (senior devs, 40+ hrs/week)
- None mention the open source / research angle
- The "coding agent" specificity gets lost in some

## New Ideas for Round 3

- Focus on the "production" angle (production AI coding)
- Focus on "professional" AI users vs. casual
- Consider: what would make someone using Claude Code 8 hrs/day excited?
