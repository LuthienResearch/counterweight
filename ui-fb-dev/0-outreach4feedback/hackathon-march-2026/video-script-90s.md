## Script

**[0:00 - 0:15] The Real Frustration**

It's 11:49 PM and I'm fighting with Claude Code. I've got a detailed CLAUDE.md, a design system, a whole requirements process. And Claude is just... ignoring it. Deleting tests, skipping rules, doing whatever it wants. I've been at this for hours.

**[0:15 - 0:35] Why This Matters Beyond Tonight**

Now here's the thing. If I can't get a coding agent to follow instructions when I'm sitting right here watching it, what happens when these systems get smarter and we're not watching? Redwood Research calls this AI control: can you keep an AI doing what you want, even when it's trying not to? That's the problem my co-founder Jai and I are working on. Not in a lab, but in real deployments, right now.

**[0:35 - 0:55] What We Built**

We built Luthien, an open-source proxy that sits between you and the AI. It logs everything and enforces your rules on every request and response. We're in Seldon Labs with Esben and Finn, turning Redwood's control research into software that actually ships. The first prototype of Luthien was built at an Apart hackathon.

**[0:55 - 1:15] The Ask**

We use Luthien to build Luthien. We're our own first users. But building this thing is really hard. There are a lot of bugs and edge cases, and we need your help. We want you to build on top of it and tell us how it breaks. Red-team our policies, build something wild, try to extract our API key. We've got Seldon Labs demo day in a few weeks, and we need to have really put this through the ringer before then.

**[1:15 - 1:25] Get Started**

One command gets you running. It's right below this video. Pick a project idea from this page, fork the repo, and jump into our Discord. Jai and I will be online all weekend.

**[1:25 - 1:30] Close**

A year ago I was attending EAGs to learn more about AI safety and volunteering for aisafety.com, looking for my place in the AI safety world. Now we're in Seldon building this. You can build something new this weekend too. See you in Discord.
